Flatbed Trailer Services

At Hunjan Transport LLC, we specialize in providing flatbed trailer transportation services, ensuring safe and efficient movement of various types of cargo across the UAE and beyond. Our flatbed trailers are designed to handle a wide range of goods, from standard shipments to oversized and heavy-duty equipment.

What is a Flatbed Trailer?

A flatbed trailer is an open-deck trailer with no sides or roof, offering maximum flexibility for loading and unloading cargo. This design makes it ideal for transporting heavy, oversized, and irregularly shaped loads that cannot fit into enclosed trailers.

Bogie Trailers Services

At Hunjan Transport LLC, we take pride in offering specialized transportation solutions, including Bogie Trailers, designed for the safe and efficient movement of heavy and oversized cargo. With our extensive industry experience and a dedicated fleet, we ensure smooth, reliable, and cost-effective transportation services across the UAE and beyond.

What is a Bogie Trailer?

A Bogie Trailer is a specialized type of trailer equipped with multiple axles to distribute weight evenly, making it ideal for transporting heavy loads, machinery, and oversized equipment. These trailers provide greater stability and maneuverability, especially when navigating challenging terrains and road conditions.

Low Bed Trailers Services

At Hunjan Transport LLC, we specialize in the transportation of heavy and oversized cargo with our Low Bed Trailers. Designed for carrying high-weight and out-of-gauge loads, our low bed trailers provide a safe and efficient solution for industries requiring the movement of heavy machinery, construction equipment, and project cargo.

Why Choose Our Low Bed Trailers?

Heavy Load Capacity: Our low bed trailers are built to accommodate high-weight machinery, industrial equipment, and specialized cargo. Optimal Safety & Stability: The lower deck height enhances stability and ensures safe transportation of tall and heavy cargo. Versatile Applications: Ideal for transporting construction machinery, generators, transformers, and other large equipment.

About Hunjan

Hunjan Transport LLC, a sister concern of DOABA General Land Transport (DGLT), has been a trusted name in land transportation since 2002. We specialize in inland trucking, handling container movements, heavy-duty equipment, machinery, and project cargo.
